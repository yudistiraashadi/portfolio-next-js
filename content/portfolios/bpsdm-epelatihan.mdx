BPSDM e-Pelatihan is an internal training management platform for Indonesia's Ministry of Public Works and Housing (PUPR). It centralizes the administration of training programs across the ministry's human resources department, replacing manual processes with a structured digital workflow.

## Key Features

1. **Training Program Management** — Create, schedule, and manage training sessions with participant enrollment tracking.
2. **Attendance & Completion Records** — Track participant attendance and issue completion certificates digitally.
3. **Instructor Management** — Manage trainer profiles, assignments, and schedules across multiple training programs.
4. **Reporting** — Generate participation and performance reports for ministry management.

## Technology

Built with PHP, MySQL, and Bootstrap. As an internal government system, the platform was designed to be straightforward and reliable, prioritizing stability and ease of use for ministry staff over cutting-edge technology.
