Bon is a mobile finance app designed specifically for Indonesian small and medium enterprises (UMKM). It helps business owners track income and expenses, manage receivables, and get a clear picture of their cash flow — all without needing accounting expertise.

## Key Features

1. **Expense & Income Tracking** — Log daily transactions quickly with categorized entries for easy review.
2. **Receivables Management** — Keep tabs on who owes you money and send reminders when payments are due.
3. **Financial Reports** — Auto-generated summaries give a snapshot of business health at a glance.
4. **Multi-business Support** — Manage finances for more than one business from a single account.

## Technology

Built with React Native for cross-platform iOS and Android support. The app was designed with simplicity as a core principle — UMKM owners often have limited time and no financial background, so every interaction was optimized to be fast and intuitive.
