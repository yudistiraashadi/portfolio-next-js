The BPSDM PUPR website is the official public-facing portal for the Human Resources Development Center (Badan Pengembangan Sumber Daya Manusia) of Indonesia's Ministry of Public Works and Housing. It serves as the central information hub for ministry HR activities, announcements, and resources.

## Key Features

1. **News & Announcements** — Publish ministry news, training schedules, and official announcements for public access.
2. **Content Management System** — Internal admin panel for ministry staff to manage all site content without technical knowledge.
3. **Document Repository** — Centralized access to ministry publications, regulations, and reference documents.
4. **Organizational Structure** — Displays the BPSDM organizational hierarchy and contact information for each unit.

## Technology

Built with Codeigniter, PHP, Bootstrap, and MySQL. The project followed government web standards while delivering a clean, maintainable codebase that ministry staff could operate independently after handover.
