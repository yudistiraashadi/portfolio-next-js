Kelasly is a mobile learning platform built for Kelasly.id, an Indonesian education startup. The app connects students with tutors and online classes, making private and group lessons more accessible across the country.

## Key Features

1. **Class Discovery** — Browse and search available classes by subject, level, and tutor rating.
2. **Tutor Profiles** — Detailed profiles with credentials, reviews, and scheduling availability.
3. **Session Booking** — Book one-on-one or group sessions directly through the app with calendar integration.
4. **Real-time Notifications** — Firebase-powered push notifications for booking confirmations, reminders, and updates.

## Technology

Built with React Native for cross-platform support and Firebase for real-time data sync, authentication, and push notifications. The architecture was designed to handle rapid onboarding of new tutors and classes as the startup scaled its marketplace.
