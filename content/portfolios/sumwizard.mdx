
Sumwizard is an AI-powered platform that converts YouTube videos and audio files into structured, searchable knowledge. Users can upload or link media content and receive automated transcriptions, concise summaries, and the ability to ask questions about the content — all powered by modern natural language processing models.

## Key Features

### 1. Automated Transcription

Processes audio and video content to produce accurate transcriptions. Supports multiple languages and handles various audio quality levels, making it suitable for interviews, lectures, podcasts, and meeting recordings.

### 2. Intelligent Summarization

Generates concise summaries that capture the key points of lengthy media content. Users can quickly understand the essence of hour-long videos without watching them in full, with summaries organized by topic and chronology.

### 3. Question Answering

An interactive Q&A feature allows users to ask specific questions about the media content. The system retrieves relevant segments and synthesizes answers grounded in the source material, enabling targeted information extraction.

### 4. YouTube Integration

Direct integration with YouTube allows users to paste a video URL and immediately begin analysis. The platform handles video downloading, audio extraction, and processing transparently.

### 5. Content Library

Processed media files are organized in a personal library where users can revisit transcriptions, summaries, and previous Q&A sessions. Search across all processed content makes it easy to find specific information.

## Purpose and Benefits

Sumwizard addresses the growing challenge of extracting value from media-heavy information sources. By automating the labor-intensive process of watching, listening, and note-taking, the platform helps researchers, students, journalists, and professionals convert audio-visual content into actionable text-based knowledge efficiently.
