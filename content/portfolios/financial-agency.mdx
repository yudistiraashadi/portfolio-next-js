
A corporate website for AAA Investama (Triple A Advisory & Investment), a private investment company and corporate finance advisory firm based in Indonesia. The site presents the company's services, expertise, and contact information through a professionally designed interface with CMS-powered content management.

## Key Features

### 1. Service Showcase

Dynamic service pages highlight the company's advisory and investment offerings. Each service has its own dedicated page with detailed descriptions, loaded from the CMS so content can be updated without code changes.

### 2. Company Profile

About-us pages present the company's mission, team, and track record. Dynamic routing allows the company to maintain multiple profile sections covering different aspects of their corporate identity.

### 3. Contact Form with Email Notifications

A contact form on the homepage allows prospective clients to reach out directly. Submissions are processed server-side and forwarded via email to the company's team, ensuring no inquiry is missed.

### 4. CMS-Powered Content

All dynamic content — services, company information, and other business data — is managed through PocketBase, a self-hosted backend. The company can update their website content independently without developer involvement.

### 5. SEO and Social Sharing

Built-in OpenGraph and Twitter card metadata ensures the site presents well when shared on social media and ranks effectively in search results, supporting the company's digital presence.

## Purpose and Benefits

The website serves as AAA Investama's digital storefront, establishing credibility and providing a professional first impression for prospective clients exploring investment and corporate finance advisory services in Indonesia.
