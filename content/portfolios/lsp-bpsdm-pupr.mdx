The LSP BPSDM PUPR website is the official portal for the Professional Certification Body (Lembaga Sertifikasi Profesi) of Indonesia's Ministry of Public Works and Housing. It provides information about professional certification programs, competency standards, and the certification process for construction and engineering professionals.

## Key Features

1. **Certification Program Catalog** — Detailed listings of all certification schemes available, including competency requirements and prerequisites.
2. **Registration Information** — Step-by-step guidance on how to apply for certification and what documents are required.
3. **Assessment Schedule** — Published schedule of upcoming competency assessments across different regions.
4. **News & Announcements** — Updates on new certification schemes, regulatory changes, and ministry news.

## Technology

Built with Codeigniter, PHP, Bootstrap, and MySQL. The site was built to meet government web accessibility standards while remaining easy for ministry staff to maintain and update through the CMS.
