
PUSPAS UNAIR is the official website for Pusat Pengelolaan Dana Sosial (Social Fund Management Center) at Universitas Airlangga in Surabaya, Indonesia. The platform facilitates online donations and provides transparency into how social funds are managed and distributed by the university.

## Key Features

### 1. Online Donation Portal

Donors can contribute to various university social programs directly through the website. The donation flow guides users through fund selection, amount entry, and payment confirmation with a streamlined interface.

### 2. Program Showcase

Each social program managed by PUSPAS has a dedicated page presenting its goals, beneficiaries, and funding progress. Prospective donors can browse active programs and choose where to direct their contributions.

### 3. Fund Transparency

Public reporting pages show how donations are allocated and utilized. This transparency builds donor confidence by demonstrating accountability in social fund management.

### 4. Donor Management

The system tracks donor information and contribution history, enabling PUSPAS to maintain relationships with supporters and provide tax receipts or acknowledgment letters as needed.

### 5. News and Updates

A content section keeps donors and the public informed about PUSPAS activities, program outcomes, and upcoming fundraising campaigns.

## Purpose and Benefits

The PUSPAS website centralizes Universitas Airlangga's social fund operations into a public-facing platform that makes donating accessible and fund management transparent. By moving donation workflows online, PUSPAS reaches a broader donor base while providing the accountability that institutional social programs require.
