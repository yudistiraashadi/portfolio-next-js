Panglima Ekspres is the official web presence for a Surabaya-based Umroh and Hajj travel agency. The site serves as the primary touchpoint for prospective pilgrims to learn about packages, register interest, and get in touch with the agency.

## Key Features

1. **Package Listings** — Detailed Umroh and Hajj package pages with itineraries, inclusions, pricing, and departure dates.
2. **Online Registration** — Prospective pilgrims can submit registration forms directly through the site.
3. **Gallery** — Photo and video gallery from previous pilgrimages to build trust with new customers.
4. **Contact & WhatsApp Integration** — Easy contact options including direct WhatsApp links for quick inquiries.

## Technology

Built with Codeigniter, PHP, MySQL, and Tailwind CSS. The design prioritized trust and clarity — pilgrimage travel requires significant financial and emotional commitment, so the site was built to feel authoritative and easy to navigate for users of all age groups.
