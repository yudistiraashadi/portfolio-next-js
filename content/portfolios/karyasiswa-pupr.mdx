
Karyasiswa PUPR Monitoring Dashboard is a web-based platform built for Indonesia's Ministry of Public Works and Housing (PUPR) to track and evaluate scholarship recipients studying domestically and abroad. The system centralizes study progress data and automates reporting workflows that were previously handled manually.

## Key Features

### 1. Study Progress Tracking

Monitors each scholarship recipient's academic milestones, including semester completion, GPA trends, and thesis progress. Supervisors can view individual timelines and flag students who fall behind schedule.

### 2. Periodic Reporting

Automates the collection of periodic progress reports from scholarship recipients. Students submit structured updates through the platform, reducing administrative overhead and ensuring consistent data quality.

### 3. Data Visualization

Interactive dashboards present aggregate statistics on scholarship utilization, completion rates, and study distributions across institutions and programs. Charts and filters allow quick analysis by cohort, field of study, or geographic region.

### 4. Evaluation Workflows

Supports structured evaluation processes where reviewers can assess student performance, provide feedback, and recommend continuation or termination of funding based on documented progress.

## Purpose and Benefits

The dashboard replaces fragmented spreadsheet-based tracking with a unified platform, giving PUPR's human resources division real-time visibility into their scholarship investment. By standardizing data collection and reporting, the system helps ensure accountability and supports data-driven decisions about the Karyasiswa program's effectiveness.
