
Traker Kediri is a mobile application for the city of Kediri, East Java, providing residents with real-time public transportation information, live CCTV feeds, and city service updates. Built in partnership with Kediri's Department of Transportation (Dishub), the app puts urban mobility tools in citizens' pockets.

## Key Features

### 1. Public Minibus Routes (Angkot)

Interactive maps display all angkot routes across the city with line-arrow visualizations showing direction of travel. Residents can plan their commutes by browsing available routes and seeing which minibus lines serve their area.

### 2. Bus Route Information

Dedicated views for bus routes complement the angkot system, giving users a complete picture of Kediri's public transit network with route details and stop locations displayed on the map.

### 3. Live CCTV Streams

Real-time video feeds from traffic cameras positioned around the city let users check road conditions before traveling. The CCTV section streams footage directly within the app, helping residents avoid congested areas.

### 4. Parking Information

A parking section provides information about available parking zones across the city, helping drivers locate parking before reaching their destination.

### 5. Public Complaint System (Pengaduan)

Citizens can submit complaints or reports about transportation issues directly through the app. This feedback channel gives the transportation department real-time insight into service quality and infrastructure problems.

### 6. City News and Updates

A news section delivers updates from the transportation department about route changes, road closures, events, and other announcements relevant to Kediri residents.

## Purpose and Benefits

Traker Kediri brings Kediri's public transportation system into the digital age by giving residents easy access to route information, real-time traffic conditions, and direct communication with the city's transportation department — all from a single mobile app available on Google Play.
