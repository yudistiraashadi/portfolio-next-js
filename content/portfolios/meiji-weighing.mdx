
The Meiji Weighing Monitoring System is a web application developed for PT Meiji Indonesian Pharmaceutical Industries, a pharmaceutical manufacturer based in Bangil, East Java. The platform digitizes and centralizes weighing data from production lines, supporting the company's commitment to GMP compliance and product quality assurance.

## Key Features

### 1. Automated Weighing Data Collection

Integrates directly with industrial weighing scales on the production floor. Weight readings are captured automatically and stored with timestamps, operator IDs, and batch references, eliminating manual transcription errors.

### 2. Real-Time Monitoring Dashboard

Displays live weighing activity across production lines in a single view. Supervisors can monitor ongoing batches, identify deviations from target weights immediately, and take corrective action before material waste occurs.

### 3. Batch Traceability

Every recorded weight measurement is linked to its corresponding production batch. This end-to-end traceability supports audit requirements and enables rapid investigation when quality discrepancies are detected downstream.

### 4. Tolerance Alerts and Validation

The system validates each measurement against predefined tolerance ranges. Out-of-spec readings trigger instant alerts to operators and supervisors, preventing non-conforming materials from advancing through the production process.

### 5. Reporting and Data Export

Generates production reports, trend analyses, and compliance summaries that can be exported for regulatory submissions and internal reviews. Historical data enables the quality team to identify recurring patterns and optimize formulation processes.

## Purpose and Benefits

By replacing paper-based weighing logs with automated, real-time digital records, the system strengthens PT Meiji's quality control processes and regulatory compliance posture. Accurate, tamper-evident data collection reduces rework, minimizes material waste, and provides the documentation trail required for pharmaceutical manufacturing standards.
