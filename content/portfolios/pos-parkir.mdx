
Pos Parkir is a parking management system built for the Tulungagung City Government in East Java, Indonesia. The platform digitizes parking operations across the city with automated vehicle detection, real-time occupancy monitoring, and revenue tracking for municipal parking zones.

## Key Features

### 1. Automated Vehicle Detection

Computer vision capabilities enable automatic license plate recognition and vehicle type classification at parking entry and exit points, reducing manual input and speeding up the parking flow.

### 2. Real-Time Occupancy Monitoring

A centralized dashboard displays live parking occupancy across managed zones. Operators can see at a glance which areas are full, approaching capacity, or have available spaces.

### 3. Transaction Management

Every parking event is logged with entry time, exit time, vehicle details, and fee calculation. The system maintains a complete transaction history for auditing and financial reconciliation.

### 4. Revenue Tracking and Reporting

Aggregated financial reports break down parking revenue by zone, time period, and vehicle type. City administrators can monitor collection performance and identify underperforming areas.

### 5. Multi-Zone Management

Supports multiple parking zones across the city, each with independent capacity tracking and fee structures. Central administrators oversee all zones while local operators manage day-to-day operations at their assigned locations.

## Purpose and Benefits

Pos Parkir replaces paper-based ticketing with a digital system that gives Tulungagung's transportation department real-time visibility into parking operations. Automated vehicle detection reduces human error, transaction logging improves revenue accountability, and centralized reporting supports data-driven decisions about urban parking infrastructure.
